A Passionate Technology Consultant, Lifelong Learner, and Explorer

I am a dynamic Tech Consultant with a focus on Duck Creek Platform Engineering and front-end development using Express 3. My journey in the IT world has been shaped by my deep expertise in the Property & Casualty (P&C) insurance domain for the US market. From requirement analysis and policy development to rating and forms, I bring a comprehensive skill set to deliver innovative and user-focused solutions. I’m proficient in leveraging tools like Duck Creek Author, Azure DevOps, JIRA, and debugging utilities such as Data Tester and Trace Monitor to ensure optimal performance and issue resolution.

Beyond my technical expertise, I am a lifelong learner who thrives on exploring new tools, technologies, and methodologies to stay ahead in a rapidly evolving industry. My tech stack includes SQL Server, Oracle, MySQL, C#, PHP, Java, HTML, CSS, ASP.NET, .NET libraries, Postman, and Git platforms, reflecting my versatility across multiple domains. I take pride in crafting intuitive user experiences and delivering solutions that align with business goals and user needs.

Outside of work, I have a vibrant and fulfilling personal life that fuels my professional journey. I am passionate about traveling, exploring new cultures, and discovering unique places. These experiences inspire me, broadening my perspective and creativity. I enjoy making meaningful connections, watching movies, and finding joy in the small moments that life offers.
