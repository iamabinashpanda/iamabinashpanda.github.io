My Hometown (Peaceful Place)

Baleshwar

also known as Balasore, is a city in the state of Odisha, about 215 km from the state capital Bhubaneswar and 300 km from Kolkata, in eastern India. It is the administrative headquarters of Baleshwar district and the largest city as well as health and educational hub of northern Odisha. Balasore is famous for its beaches, temples, wildlife sanctuaries, and missile testing site. The most loved attractions in Balasore are Sari Sua Waterfalls, Chandipur Beach and Balasore Bichitrapur Mangrove Sanctuary.
